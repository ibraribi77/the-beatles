FACTS

  • The Beatles have spent 175 weeks at number one on the charts.
  • He Beatles album with the longest consecutive time spent at
    number one is their debut album Please Please Me at 30 weeks.
  • The Beatles’ first single in 1962 was “Love Me Do.” In 2012,
    the song became Public Domain in Europe.
  • The last album recorded by the Beatles was Abbey Road.
    The last album released was Let It Be.
  • The Beatles earned $90,000 in 35 minutes for their
    Minneapolis show in August 1965.
